Top Neighborhoods in Torrance

Torrance is home to several unique neighborhoods, each offering its own charm and amenities. Here are some of the most sought-after areas:

  • Southwood: Southwood is a family-friendly neighborhood with tree-lined streets and a welcoming community atmosphere.   • Old Torrance: Old Torrance is a historic district that showcases homes with beautiful vintage architecture. The neighborhood is very walkable, with easy access to charming local shops, cozy restaurants, and relaxing parks. It’s a great place to enjoy a mix of history and modern convenience.
  • Hollywood Riviera: Hollywood Riviera is a stunning neighborhood near the coast, known for its breathtaking ocean views. The area features luxurious homes, making it ideal for those who enjoy an upscale lifestyle. Residents appreciate the blend of beachside living and peaceful surroundings.
  • West Torrance: West Torrance stands out for its excellent schools and abundance of parks, making it a favorite for families. The homes here vary from classic mid-century designs to sleek, modern constructions. This neighborhood offers a safe and vibrant community environment for residents.
  • North Torrance: North Torrance is a more affordable neighborhood with easy access to major freeways, making it perfect for commuters. The area provides a good mix of convenience and value for families or individuals. It’s a practical choice for those who want a balance between affordability and accessibility.
